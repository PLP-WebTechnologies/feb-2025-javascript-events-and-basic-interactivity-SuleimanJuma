Blockchain

What is Blockchain?

Imagine a digital notebook that:

  • ✔️ Everyone can see (transparent)
  • ✔️ No one can erase (tamper-proof)
  • ✔️ Automatically updates (no middlemen)
How It Works (Like a Team Game)
  • You make a move (e.g., send crypto, sign a contract)
  • Thousands of computers worldwide check if it’s valid
  • Once approved, it’s added to a "block" and chained to past blocks
Why It’s Useful
  • Money Transfers: Send funds globally without banks (faster/cheaper)
  • Smart Contracts: Rent a car or house without paperwork (auto-payments)
  • Ownership Proof: Verify luxury goods, tickets, or digital art (NFTs)
Everyday Examples
  • Bitcoin: Digital cash with no government control
  • Property Records: Instant home sale verifications
  • Vaccine Tracking: Safe supply chains for medicines
